Transforming E-Cigarette Waste into Educational Opportunities

About the Project

EcoPuff is dedicated to tackling electronic waste by transforming discarded e-cigarettes into valuable educational kits. Our primary audience includes schools and social institutions interested in purchasing or renting these kits. Each kit comes with comprehensive guides, empowering students and participants in social projects to assemble various useful items and develop crucial technological skills. We aim to make a positive social impact by fostering innovation and learning, while also contributing to a healthier environment.

How It Works

Responsible Collection

We focus on establishing effective channels for collecting discarded e-cigarettes to ensure a steady supply for recycling.

Safe Disassembly

E-cigarettes are carefully disassembled, separating components like batteries, plastic, and electronics for proper processing.

Technical Recycling

Recovered materials are processed and recycled, preparing them for a new life as components in our educational kits.

Educational Reuse

Recycled components are assembled into kits designed for educational projects, such as rechargeable LED lanterns or power banks, complete with assembly guides.

Benefits

Significant Reduction of E-Waste

Smart Reuse of Materials

Promoting a Circular Economy

Fostering Tech Skills & Environmental Awareness
